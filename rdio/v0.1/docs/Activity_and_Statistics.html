Rdio API: Activity and Statistics

These methods are relative to the tag 'Activity and Statistics'

getActivityStream

Gets the activity events for a user, a user's friends, or everyone on Rdio.

getHeavyRotation

Finds the most popular artists or albums for a user, their friends or the whole site.

getNewReleases

Returns new albums released across a timeframe.

getTopCharts

Returns the site-wide most popular items for a given type.
